Convert i686 linker from SysV .hash to .gnu.hash for consistency with x86/RISC-V.

The i686 linker uses a SysV .hash section (DT_HASH) while x86-64 and RISC-V linkers
use .gnu.hash (DT_GNU_HASH). This is the most important linker cohesion discrepancy.

Changes:
1. Replace build_sysv_hash() with build_gnu_hash_32() in i686/linker/elf.rs
2. Change DT_HASH to DT_GNU_HASH in the .dynamic section
3. Rename section from .hash to .gnu.hash with SHT_GNU_HASH type
4. Update section layout and comments to match

Status: in_progress
