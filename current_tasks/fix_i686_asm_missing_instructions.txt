Fix i686 native assembler missing instruction encodings

The i686 assembler (MY_ASM=builtin) fails on ~51 tests that pass with GCC assembler.
Main issues:
1. cvttss2si/cvtsd2si without 'l' suffix not recognized (10 tests)
2. addl with SymbolMod immediates (NTPOFF, etc.) not handled (7 tests)
3. Missing instructions: fsqrt, fnstsw, f2xm1, cpuid, lock prefix,
   pslldq, punpcklbw, paddd, paddw, psubusb, movnti, bt reg->mem,
   movq for MMX, syscall
4. shrl with implicit %cl register (3 tests)
