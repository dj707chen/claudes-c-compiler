Implement direct ld invocation for AArch64 when MY_LD is set.

This mirrors the x86-64 implementation (link_direct_ld) but for the ARM backend:
- find_gcc_lib_dir_aarch64(): probe GCC cross-compiler dirs for crtbegin.o
- find_crt_dir_aarch64(): probe system CRT dirs for crt1.o/crti.o/crtn.o
- link_direct_ld_aarch64(): replicate GCC's collect2 behavior for aarch64
- Wire up EM_AARCH64 (183) dispatch in link_with_args()

Status: in_progress
