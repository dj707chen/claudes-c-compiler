Task: Add macro expansion tracing to diagnostics
Status: in_progress
Started: 2026-01-30

Goal: When an error/warning occurs inside a macro expansion, emit GCC-style
"note: in expansion of macro 'X'" diagnostic notes showing the expansion chain.

Approach:
1. Preprocessor emits special markers when entering/leaving macro expansions
2. SourceManager parses these markers and builds a macro expansion map
3. DiagnosticEngine queries the expansion map and renders macro trace notes

Key files:
- src/frontend/preprocessor/macro_defs.rs (emit markers)
- src/common/source.rs (parse markers, build expansion map)
- src/common/error.rs (render macro expansion traces)
